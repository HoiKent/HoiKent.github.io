Auriea Harvey Reading Response

Auriea Harvey is a digital artist and game designer who has made significant contributions to the field of digital sculpture. She co-founded the artist collective Tale of Tales and is best known for her work on the video game "The Path." Harvey's approach to digital sculpture involves using software to create three-dimensional models that can be manipulated and explored in virtual environments. She sees digital sculpture as a way to push the boundaries of traditional sculpture, allowing artists to create complex, interactive works that can be experienced in new and exciting ways. In addition to her work on "The Path," Harvey has created numerous other digital sculptures and installations. She has also been involved in education, teaching courses on digital sculpture and game design at various universities. Harvey believes that the future of digital sculpture is bright, and that advancements in technology will continue to push the boundaries of what is possible. She sees virtual and augmented reality as particularly promising areas for digital sculpture, as they allow for even more immersive and interactive experiences. Overall, Auriea Harvey's work and vision for the future of digital sculpture highlight the exciting possibilities that technology can offer to artists and audiences alike.
